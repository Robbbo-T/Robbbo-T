## Component Structure

Each Component Area (CA) contains multiple Component Implementations (CI), and each CI has 11 lifecycle phases:

1. **01-Requirements** - Requirements definition and traceability
2. **02-Design** - Design specifications and architecture  
3. **03-Building-Prototyping** - Development and prototyping
4. **04-Executables-Packages** - Compiled code and deliverables
5. **05-Verification-Validation** - V&V activities and test results
6. **06-Integration-Qualification** - Integration testing and qualification
7. **07-Certification-Security** - Certification evidence and security
8. **08-Production-Scale** - Production deployment and scaling
9. **09-Ops-Services** - Operational procedures and service management
10. **10-MRO** - Maintenance, repair, and overhaul procedures
11. **11-Sustainment-Recycle** - End-of-life and sustainability planning
