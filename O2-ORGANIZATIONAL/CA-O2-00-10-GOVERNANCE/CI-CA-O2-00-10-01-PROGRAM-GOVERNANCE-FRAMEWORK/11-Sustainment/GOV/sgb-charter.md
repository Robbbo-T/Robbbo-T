## Sustainment Governance Board Charter

### 1. Purpose and Mission
The Sustainment Governance Board (SGB) provides strategic oversight and governance for the complete lifecycle sustainment of the AMPEL360 H₂-BWB-Q aircraft, ensuring cost-effective operations, maintenance, and eventual disposal while maintaining safety and performance standards.

### 2. Scope and Authority

**Sustainment Scope:**
- Operational support and logistics
- Maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) oversight
- Configuration and modification management
- Performance monitoring and improvement
- End-of-life planning and disposal
- Obsolescence management
- Technology refresh decisions

**Decision Authority:**
- Sustainment strategy approval
- Lifecycle cost optimization
- Technology insertion authorization
- Disposal planning and execution
- Resource allocation for sustainment activities
