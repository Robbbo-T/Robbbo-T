# Lifecycle Phase Documentation

This directory contains documentation and artifacts for this specific lifecycle phase of the component.

## Purpose
This phase represents one of the 11 main lifecycle phases in the OPTIME framework:

1. **01-Requirements** - Requirements definition and traceability
2. **02-Design** - Design specifications and architecture
3. **03-Building-Prototyping** - Development and prototyping activities
4. **04-Executables-Packages** - Compiled code, packages, and deliverables
5. **05-Verification-Validation** - V&V activities and test results
6. **06-Integration-Qualification** - Integration testing and qualification
7. **07-Certification-Security** - Certification evidence and security validation
8. **08-Production-Scale** - Production deployment and scaling
9. **09-Ops-Services** - Operational procedures and service management
10. **10-MRO** - Maintenance, repair, and overhaul procedures
11. **11-Sustainment-Recycle** - End-of-life and sustainability planning

## Standards & Compliance
Refer to the parent component documentation for applicable standards and regulations.

## Digital Evidence Twin (DET)
All activities in this phase should produce appropriate evidence for the DET system.
