# CA-O-006-STRATEGIC-ALIGNMENT

## Overview
Strategic alignment framework ensuring program vision, mission, objectives, and roadmap milestones align with hydrogen aviation development goals and market requirements.

## Configuration Items (CIs)

### CI-CA-O-006-001-VISION-MISSION
Program vision and mission statements aligned with sustainable aviation goals, hydrogen propulsion advancement, and blended wing body innovation.

### CI-CA-O-006-002-STRATEGIC-OBJECTIVES
Measurable strategic objectives covering technical performance, certification timeline, market positioning, and sustainability targets for the hydrogen BWB configuration.

### CI-CA-O-006-003-ROADMAP-MILESTONES
Detailed program roadmap with key milestones, decision gates, and deliverables aligned with certification requirements and market entry strategy.

## Standards Compliance
- ISO 21500 for strategic project management
- AS9100 for aerospace strategic planning
- Energy-as-Policy (EAP) alignment with sustainability objectives
- Market and regulatory alignment for hydrogen aviation certification
