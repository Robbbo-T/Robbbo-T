# CA-O-005-STEERING-COMMITTEES

## Overview
Steering committee framework providing technical oversight, safety governance, and certification guidance for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN program.

## Configuration Items (CIs)

### CI-CA-O-005-001-TECHNICAL-BOARD
Technical steering board providing oversight for design decisions, technology selection, and integration approaches across the 15 AMEDEO-PELLICCIA domains.

### CI-CA-O-005-002-SAFETY-BOARD
Safety steering board ensuring compliance with safety standards, reviewing safety assessments, and approving safety-critical design decisions.

### CI-CA-O-005-003-CERTIFICATION-BOARD
Certification steering board coordinating with regulatory authorities, managing certification strategy, and ensuring compliance with airworthiness requirements.

## Standards Compliance
- ARP4754A for technical oversight requirements
- ARP4761A for safety assessment governance
- CS-25 for certification board authority
- DO-178C/254 for software/hardware oversight
- AS9100 for aerospace governance standards
