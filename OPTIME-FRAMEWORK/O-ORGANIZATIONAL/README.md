# O-ORGANIZATIONAL

## Overview
The Organizational layer of the OPTIME framework defines governance structures, financial controls, KPI monitoring, organizational structure, steering committees, and strategic alignment for the AEROSPACE DIGITAL TWIN READINESS FRAMEWORK - PROGRAM AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN.

## Structure

### CA-O-001-GOVERNANCE
Program governance framework, quality management systems, and certification roadmap.

### CA-O-002-FINANCIAL-CONTROL  
Budget tracking, lifecycle cost modeling, and funding strategy management.

### CA-O-003-KPI-MONITORING
Performance indicators, risk dashboards, and traceability metrics.

### CA-O-004-ORGANIZATIONAL-STRUCTURE
Organizational charts, roles & responsibilities, and decision rights matrix.

### CA-O-005-STEERING-COMMITTEES
Technical board, safety board, and certification board governance.

### CA-O-006-STRATEGIC-ALIGNMENT
Vision & mission alignment, strategic objectives, and roadmap milestones.

## Purpose
Establishes the foundational organizational structures and governance mechanisms required for successful execution of the AMPEL360 H2 BWB program while ensuring compliance with aerospace industry standards and regulations.
