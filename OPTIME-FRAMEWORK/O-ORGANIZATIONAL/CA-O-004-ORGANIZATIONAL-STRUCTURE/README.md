# CA-O-004-ORGANIZATIONAL-STRUCTURE

## Overview
Organizational structure framework defining reporting relationships, roles and responsibilities, and decision-making authority for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN program.

## Configuration Items (CIs)

### CI-CA-O-004-001-ORG-CHART
Hierarchical organizational chart defining reporting relationships, team structures, and functional dependencies across all program domains and phases.

### CI-CA-O-004-002-ROLES-RESPONSIBILITIES
Detailed RACI matrix and role definitions for all positions within the program, ensuring clear accountability and responsibility assignment.

### CI-CA-O-004-003-DECISION-RIGHTS
Decision authority matrix defining approval levels, escalation paths, and decision-making rights across technical, commercial, and operational domains.

## Standards Compliance
- AS9100 for aerospace organizational requirements
- ISO 21500 for project management organizational frameworks
- ARP4754A for systems engineering organizational responsibilities
- Cross-domain coordination protocols for AMEDEO-PELLICCIA 15-domain architecture
