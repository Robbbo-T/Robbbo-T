# E1-ENVIRONMENTAL_REMEDIATION_CIRCULARITY

## Overview
Environmental remediation and circularity domain for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN aircraft, encompassing environmental control systems, safety systems, waste management, noise reduction, and sustainable lifecycle management specifically addressing hydrogen aircraft environmental challenges.

## Domain Scope
This domain covers **environmental systems and sustainability**:
- Cabin air systems and environmental controls
- Ice and rain protection systems
- Fire detection and suppression
- Hydrogen leak detection and safety venting
- Water and waste management systems
- Noise and emission control
- Circularity and end-of-life management
- Ground environmental safety

## Constituent Assemblies (CAs)

### CA-E1-001-ECS-CABIN-AIR-SYSTEM
Environmental Control System components including heat exchangers, air mixing manifolds, HEPA/ULPA filtration, and air distribution systems.

### CA-E1-002-PRESSURIZATION-VENTILATION
Pressurization and ventilation systems including outflow valves, safety relief valves, pressure sensors, and controlled leakage paths.

### CA-E1-003-ICE-AND-RAIN-PROTECTION
Ice and rain protection systems including electrical thermal mats, deicing boots, piccolo tube manifolds, and drainage systems.

### CA-E1-004-FIRE-DETECTION-SUPPRESSION
Fire detection and suppression systems including detection loops, fire bottles, discharge lines, and cargo bay suppression geometries.

### CA-E1-005-H2-LEAK-VENTING-PURGE-SAFETY
Hydrogen leak detection sensors, vent masts, purge systems, zone classification, and gas sampling ports for hydrogen safety.

### CA-E1-006-WATER-WASTE-SYSTEMS
Potable water systems, waste management, drain systems, grey water recovery, and antimicrobial treatments.

### CA-E1-007-NOISE-EMISSIONS-CONTRAIL-MGMT
Acoustic liners, noise suppression, trailing edge modifications, and contrail management systems for environmental impact reduction.

### CA-E1-008-THERMAL-MANAGEMENT-NONCRYO
Non-cryogenic thermal management including cooling loops, heat exchangers, ram air systems, and thermal interface materials.

### CA-E1-009-REMEDIATION-CIRCULARITY-LIFECYCLE
Material passports, design for disassembly, recycling strategies, adhesive debonding, and end-of-life sorting systems.

### CA-E1-010-BIOSECURITY-AIR-QUALITY
Advanced air quality systems including UV-C integration, enhanced filtration, antimicrobial surfaces, and odor management.

### CA-E1-011-DRAINS-CONDENSATE-ICE-MGMT
Condensate drain networks, vapor barriers, ice prevention systems, and water ingress protection systems.

### CA-E1-012-GROUND-ENV-SAFETY-REMEDIATION
Ground environmental safety including spill containment, stormwater control, deicing fluid collection, and hazmat storage.

## Domain Boundaries

### What E1-ENVIRONMENTAL includes:
- **Environmental control systems and ducting**
- **Safety and protection systems (fire, ice, H₂)**
- **Waste and water management**
- **Noise and emission control**
- **Sustainability and circularity**
- **Environmental monitoring and protection**

### What E1-ENVIRONMENTAL does NOT include:
- **E2-ENERGY**: Electrical power systems and energy generation
- **E3-ELECTRONICS**: Electronic controls and sensors (hardware)
- **C2-CRYOGENICS**: Cryogenic hydrogen systems
- **M-MECHANICAL**: Pure mechanical actuators and mechanisms

## Cross-Domain Integration
- **↔ E2**: Power for environmental systems
- **↔ E3**: Environmental sensors and control electronics
- **↔ M**: Mechanical mounting and valve actuation
- **↔ C2**: Hydrogen safety integration
- **↔ D**: Safety and cybersecurity frameworks
