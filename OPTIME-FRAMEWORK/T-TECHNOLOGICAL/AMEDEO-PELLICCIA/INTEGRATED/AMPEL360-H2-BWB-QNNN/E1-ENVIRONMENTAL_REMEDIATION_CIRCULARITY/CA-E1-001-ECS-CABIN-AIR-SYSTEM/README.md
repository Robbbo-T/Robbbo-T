# CA-E1-001-ECS-CABIN-AIR-SYSTEM

## Overview
Environmental Control System (ECS) cabin air management for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN configuration, providing clean, conditioned air for passenger and crew comfort while addressing hydrogen safety requirements.

## Configuration Items (CIs)

### CI-CA-E1-001-001-ECS-HEAT-EXCHANGER-CORES
Primary and secondary heat exchanger cores for air conditioning with enhanced efficiency designs optimized for hydrogen aircraft thermal management integration.

### CI-CA-E1-001-002-ECS-MIX-MANIFOLD-DUCTS
Air mixing manifolds and distribution ducting systems designed for BWB geometry with optimized flow patterns and hydrogen leak detection integration.

### CI-CA-E1-001-003-ECS-RECIRC-HEPA-ULPA-MODULES
High-Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) and Ultra-Low Particulate Air (ULPA) filtration modules with enhanced hydrogen contamination detection capabilities.

### CI-CA-E1-001-004-ECS-RECIRC-FANS-MOUNTS
Recirculation fan assemblies and mounting systems with redundancy provisions and hydrogen-compatible materials and sealing systems.

### CI-CA-E1-001-005-ECS-RAM-AIR-INLET-OUTLET-GEOM
Ram air inlet and outlet geometries optimized for BWB configuration with minimal drag impact and enhanced hydrogen purge integration capabilities.

### CI-CA-E1-001-006-ECS-BYPASS-DOORS-LOUVERS
Bypass door and louver systems for airflow control with rapid response capabilities for hydrogen emergency scenarios and normal operational modes.

## Cross-Domain Interfaces
- **↔ E2**: Power distribution for ECS components and thermal management
- **↔ E3**: Control systems and environmental sensors
- **↔ M**: Mechanical mounting and actuator interfaces
- **↔ C2**: Integration with hydrogen leak detection and purge systems

## Standards Compliance
- CS-25 for environmental systems requirements
- RTCA DO-160 for environmental qualification
- ASHRAE standards for air quality and thermal comfort
- Hydrogen safety standards for contamination detection
- Energy-as-Policy (EAP) efficiency optimization
