# CA-M-001-FLIGHT-CONTROL-MECHANISMS

## Overview
Flight control mechanisms for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN configuration, covering elevon, flaperon, spoiler drive systems, and associated mechanical components for the blended wing body control surface architecture.

## Configuration Items (CIs)

### CI-CA-M-001-001-FC-ELEVON-HINGE-LINES
Elevon hinge line mechanisms optimized for BWB configuration with distributed load paths and enhanced aerodynamic efficiency for combined elevator/aileron functionality.

### CI-CA-M-001-002-FC-FLAPERON-DRIVE-BOXES
Flaperon drive box assemblies providing high-authority control surface actuation with integrated mechanical backup systems and position feedback mechanisms.

### CI-CA-M-001-003-FC-SPOILER-DRIVE-UNITS
Spoiler drive units for lift dumping and roll control with rapid deployment capabilities and mechanical locks for ground and emergency configurations.

### CI-CA-M-001-004-FC-TORQUE-TUBES-AND-GEARTRAINS
Torque tube and geartrain assemblies distributing control inputs across the wide BWB span with mechanical advantage optimization and backlash elimination.

### CI-CA-M-001-005-FC-BALANCE-MASSES
Control surface balance masses and tabs minimizing control forces and preventing flutter across the flight envelope including hydrogen weight distribution effects.

### CI-CA-M-001-006-FC-SURFACE-MECHANICAL-LOCKS
Mechanical locking mechanisms for control surfaces during maintenance, ground operations, and emergency scenarios with fail-safe engagement principles.

### CI-CA-M-001-007-FC-OVERCENTER-FAILSAFE-LINKS
Overcenter linkage mechanisms providing failsafe operation and preventing control surface hardover conditions with mechanical stops and force limiting.

### CI-CA-M-001-008-FC-LVDT-RVDT-MOUNT-KITS
Linear and Rotary Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT/RVDT) mounting systems for precise position feedback with environmental protection and calibration access.

## Cross-Domain Interfaces
