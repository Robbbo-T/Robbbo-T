# E2-ENERGY_AND_RENEWABLE

## Overview
Energy and renewable systems domain for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN aircraft, covering electrical power architecture, hydrogen energy systems, battery management, fuel cells, and renewable energy integration for sustainable aviation.

## Domain Scope
This domain covers **energy generation, distribution, and management**:
- Electrical power architecture (HVDC/MVDC/LVDC)
- Hydrogen fuel cell systems and energy conversion
- Battery management and energy storage
- Power distribution networks and protection
- Energy optimization and renewable integration
- Thermal management for energy systems

## Key Focus Areas
- Hydrogen-electric propulsion energy systems
- High-voltage DC power distribution
- Energy-as-Policy (EAP) implementation
- Renewable energy integration strategies
- Energy storage and management systems
- Power electronics and conversion systems

## Cross-Domain Interfaces
- **↔ P**: Propulsion system power requirements
- **↔ E3**: Power electronics and control systems
- **↔ M**: Mechanical actuation power
- **↔ C2**: Cryogenic hydrogen energy systems
- **↔ E1**: Environmental system power needs

## Standards Compliance
- DO-160 for aircraft electrical systems
- RTCA DO-311 for electrical power systems
- Hydrogen energy standards (SAE ARP6883)
- Energy-as-Policy (EAP) optimization frameworks
- UTCS-MI v5.0+ traceability for energy components
