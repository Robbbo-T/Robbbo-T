# C2-CRYOGENICS_QUANTUM_INTERFACES_HYDROGEN_CELLS

## Overview
Cryogenics, quantum interfaces, and hydrogen fuel cell domain for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN aircraft, managing ultra-low temperature systems, quantum computing interfaces, and hydrogen energy conversion systems.

## Domain Scope
This domain covers **cryogenic and quantum systems**:
- Liquid hydrogen storage and management
- Cryogenic temperature control systems
- Hydrogen fuel cell technology
- Quantum computing interfaces (non-flight-critical)
- Superconducting systems and components
- Ultra-low temperature instrumentation

## Key Focus Areas
- Cryogenic hydrogen storage tanks
- Hydrogen fuel cell systems
- Quantum Abstraction Layer (QAL) interfaces
- Superconducting electrical systems
- Cryogenic instrumentation and control
- Hydrogen safety and leak detection

## Cross-Domain Interfaces
- **↔ E2**: Energy conversion and distribution
- **↔ E1**: Hydrogen safety and environmental systems
- **↔ P**: Propulsion system hydrogen supply
- **↔ E3**: Cryogenic sensors and electronics
- **↔ D**: Quantum security interfaces

## Standards Compliance
- Hydrogen safety standards (ISO 15916)
- Cryogenic system standards (ASME BPVC)
- Quantum system containment requirements
- Pressure vessel codes for hydrogen storage
- UTCS-MI v5.0+ traceability for cryogenic systems
