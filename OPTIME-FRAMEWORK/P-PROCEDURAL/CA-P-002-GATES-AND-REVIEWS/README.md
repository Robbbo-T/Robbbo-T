# CA-P-002-GATES-AND-REVIEWS

## Overview
Structured gate and review process framework ensuring systematic progression through program phases with appropriate technical, safety, and certification oversight for the AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN program.

## Configuration Items (CIs)

### CI-CA-P-002-001-SRR-GATE
System Requirements Review (SRR) gate procedures, entry/exit criteria, deliverables, and approval workflows for validating system-level requirements and architecture.

### CI-CA-P-002-002-PDR-GATE
Preliminary Design Review (PDR) gate procedures ensuring design approach validation, interface definitions, and preliminary safety assessments are complete.

### CI-CA-P-002-003-CDR-GATE
Critical Design Review (CDR) gate procedures validating detailed design completion, manufacturing readiness, and comprehensive safety analysis.

### CI-CA-P-002-004-TRR-GATE
Test Readiness Review (TRR) gate procedures ensuring test procedures, facilities, and acceptance criteria are established before formal testing phases.

### CI-CA-P-002-005-FRR-ORR-GATES
Flight Readiness Review (FRR) and Operational Readiness Review (ORR) gate procedures ensuring airworthiness and operational readiness before service entry.

## Standards Compliance
- ARP4754A for systems engineering review gates
- ARP4761A for safety assessment reviews
- CS-25 for certification review requirements
- AS9100 for aerospace review standards
