# OPTIME FRAMEWORK v1.0
## AEROSPACE DIGITAL TWIN READINESS FRAMEWORK
### PROGRAM AMPEL360 H2 BWB QNNN

## Overview
The OPTIME (Optimization, Procedural, Technological, Intelligent, Machine, Executing) framework provides a comprehensive digital twin readiness structure for the AMPEL360 hydrogen BWB aircraft program.

## Framework Structure

### Six Main Layers

#### O-ORGANIZATIONAL
Governance structures, financial controls, KPI monitoring, organizational structure, steering committees, and strategic alignment.

#### P-PROCEDURAL  
Comprehensive processes, procedures, and workflows including safety certification, quality management, change control, and operational handover procedures.

#### T-TECHNOLOGICAL
AMEDEO-PELLICCIA 15-domain decomposition with detailed Constituent Assemblies (CAs) and Configuration Items (CIs):

**Canonical Path**: `OPTIME-FRAMEWORK/T-TECHNOLOGICAL/AMEDEO-PELLICCIA/INTEGRATED/AMPEL360-H2-BWB-QNNN/`

**Implemented Domains**:
- **A-ARCHITECTURES_AIRFRAMES_AERODYNAMICS/** - Structural architecture, aerodynamics, and airframe design
- **M-MECHANICAL_AND_CONTROL/** - Flight control mechanisms, actuation, hydraulics, landing gear, and mechanical systems
- **E1-ENVIRONMENTAL_REMEDIATION_CIRCULARITY/** - Environmental controls, cabin air systems, ice protection, fire systems, and circularity
- **D-DEFENCE_CYBERSECURITY_SAFETY/** - Platform survivability, cybersecurity, safety engineering, and regulatory compliance

#### I-INTELLIGENT
Autonomy functions with proposal-only capabilities and gated execution mechanisms.

#### M-MACHINE
Automation, machine learning static operations, simulation frameworks, and runtime environments.

#### E-EXECUTING
Operational runtime, mode management, telemetry collection, and Digital Evidence Twin (DET) operations.

## Key Features

### Structured Decomposition
- **Constituent Assemblies (CAs)**: Major functional groupings within each domain
- **Configuration Items (CIs)**: Specific components and interfaces within each CA
- **Traceability**: Full UTCS-MI compliance for requirements and evidence management

### Standards Compliance
- **Airworthiness**: ARP4754A/4761A, CS-25, DO-178C/254/326A
- **Quality**: AS9100, ISO 9001
- **Cybersecurity**: DO-326A, ISO 21434, IEC 62443
- **Data Management**: SBOM/MBOM/DBOM, DET (WORM evidence)

### Integration Points
- **Energy-as-Policy (EAP)**: Measurable energy and CO₂ optimization targets
- **Digital Evidence Twin (DET)**: Comprehensive evidence packaging and retention
- **Cross-Domain Interfaces**: Clear boundaries and interaction protocols between domains
